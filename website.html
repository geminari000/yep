Dogs

Dogs are great pets, so many people don't want them to die.

People want their dogs to live for as long as possible

Look at the table to see how long a dog might live for

Average Dog Lifespan
Small Breeds Medium Breeds Large Breeds "Giant Breeds"
10-15 Years 10-13 years 10-12 years 8-10 years

If you're curious, here are some examples of small, medium, large, and giant breeds.

  1. Small Breeds
    • Chihuahua (15-17 years)
    • Pomeranian (14-16 years)
    • Russell Terrier (12-14 years)
    • Yorkshire Terrier (12-15 years)
  2. Medium Breeds
    • Cocker Spaniel (13-15 years)
    • Poodle (12-15 years)
    • Bulldog (10-12 years)
    • Boxer (10-12 years)
    • French Bulldog (11-13 years)
  3. Large Breeds
    • German Shepherds
    • Golden Retrievers
    • Labrador Retrievers
    • Rottweilers
    • Doberman Pinschers
  4. Giant Breeds
    • Great Danes
    • St. Bernards
    • Mastiffs
